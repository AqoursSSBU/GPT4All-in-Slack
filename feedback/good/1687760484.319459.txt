how do i make pesto

To make pesto, you will need the following ingredients:
-1 cup of fresh basil leaves
-1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
-1/4 cup of pine nuts
-2 cloves of garlic, minced
-1 tablespoon of olive oil

Here's how to make it:
1. In a blender or food processor, combine the basil leaves, parmesan cheese, pine nuts, and garlic.
2. With the machine running, slowly add in the olive oil until the mixture is well combined.
3. Transfer the pesto to an airtight container and refrigerate for up to one week.
4. To serve, you can use it as a pasta sauce or spread it on sandwiches or crostini. Enjoy!
